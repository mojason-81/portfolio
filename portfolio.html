An Unfinished Read

Books I still havent' read.

I needed inspiration for something to build and happened to be staring at the bookshelf next to my desk when I noticed it was full of books I never finished. I thought that maybe someone out there might like to laugh at how terrible I am at finishing a book.....and so this page was conceived. I'm still in labor trying to push it out so please don't pay attention to the red borders. They're just a nice visual aid in seeing how things are lining up on the page. I hope you enjoy perusing some of my failed endevours. 😊
